# Privacy Policy — La Flor Blanca Labor Alert System

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview
This is a closed internal business alert system used exclusively by the owner and management staff of La Flor Blanca restaurant. It is not a public-facing service.

## Data Collected
- Employee shift times and hourly wage data (sourced from Square POS)
- Daily sales totals (sourced from Square POS)
- Recipient phone numbers (manually configured by the restaurant owner)

## How Data Is Used
Data is used solely to calculate labor cost percentages and generate internal business alerts sent via SMS to the restaurant owner and designated management staff.

## Data Sharing
No data is shared with third parties. Data is not sold, rented, or disclosed to any outside party.

## SMS Messaging
SMS alerts are sent only to phone numbers manually configured by the restaurant owner. Recipients are the owner and management staff who have agreed to receive these alerts. To opt out, contact the restaurant owner directly.
