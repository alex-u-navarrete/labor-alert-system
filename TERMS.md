# Terms and Conditions — La Flor Blanca Labor Alert System

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview
This is a closed internal SMS alert system operated by La Flor Blanca restaurant for internal business management purposes only.

## Recipients
SMS messages are sent exclusively to the restaurant owner and management staff whose phone numbers have been manually configured into the system by the owner.

## Message Frequency
Messages are sent only when labor cost thresholds are exceeded during business hours, and once weekly as a summary report. Frequency varies but will not exceed 6 messages per day.

## Message and Data Rates
Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

## Opt-Out
To stop receiving messages, contact the restaurant owner to have your number removed from the system configuration.

## Help
For questions about this system, contact the restaurant owner directly.
